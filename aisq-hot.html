How to Upgrade

Each card is worth a certain amount of credits. You'll need to redeem enough cards to cover your upgrade cost.

You can only advance one level at a time. To reach level 2, you must hold a level 1 NFT. Once an upgrade is complete, you are then eligible to upgrade to the next level.
  1. Obtain $CHAOS & Upgrade Cards
  2. Open an upgrade request ticket in Discord
  3. Follow the instructions within the ticket. (You will be asked to send your Upgrade Cards and $CHAOS)
  1. Await confirmation from the team. Once confirmed, your upgrade may take up to 3 working days to fulfill.
  2. Take it to the next level to obtain more benefits!
